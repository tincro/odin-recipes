Lasagna

Description

Here is a description of the tasty lasagna that you will enjoy at home.

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 6 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 medium onions
  • 14 oz. cottage cheese
  • 1/2 lb mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Cook tomato sauce
  3. cook onions, then put them in with cottage cheese
  4. after boiling lasagna pasta, layer sauce and cheese, then pasta
  5. Cook on 350 degrees for 45 minutes. top with mozzarella and cook for another 15 minutes.
